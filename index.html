Why Layer 2 matters

Fast, affordable Web3 infrastructure

⚡

Lower fees

Arbitrum bundles many transactions off-chain and submits them efficiently to Ethereum, reducing cost for users.

🔒

Ethereum security

Layer 2 inherits security from Ethereum, which means faster execution without sacrificing trust.

🌐

Scalable dApps

Apps can grow without the congestion of mainnet, enabling smoother NFT minting, DeFi, and gaming experiences.

Why Ethereum needed Layer 2

The problem with high traffic

Ethereum is secure but gets expensive when many users interact with it. High gas fees and slow confirmations make everyday Web3 use harder for crowds.

What Arbitrum is

Rollup-based scaling

Arbitrum is a Rollup that executes transactions off-chain and posts only the summary to Ethereum. It keeps Ethereum as the trust layer while improving speed and cost.

Real-world benefit

Cheaper and faster DeFi

Using Arbitrum instead of Ethereum mainnet can reduce transaction fees by a large margin, making DeFi swaps, NFT trades, and DApp interactions practical for more users.
